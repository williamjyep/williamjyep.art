Hi, I'm William

Growing up in the Bay Area, art lessons were overpriced and overcrowded, so I was mostly self-taught. But as I grew, I've learned how important it is to have a teacher, so I hope to provide affordable & quality art lessons for the youth in my community.

My Art Lessons

I've taught art classes over for two years at Enlighten Heart afterschool, and led my own group lessons for students in elementary to middle school.

我在 Enlighten Heart 课后项目教授艺术课程已有两年多，并且一直为小学到初中阶段的学生带领自己的小组艺术课程。

I teach students the fundamentals of drawing from observation. Each student works on their own personalized projects based on their interests and experince level.

我教授学生从观察中进行绘画的基础技巧。每位学生都会根据自己的兴趣和经验水平，完成个性化的创作项目。

All materials are provided to students and included the lesson fee ($20/hr). Classes are held in a comfertable learing environment. To learn more, please visit the "Classes" page above!

所有课程材料均由老师提供，并已包含在课程费用中($20/hr)。课堂在舒适、适合学习的环境中进行。要了解更多信息，请访问上方的“Classes”页面!
